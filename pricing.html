How PyPay Pricing Works

At PyPay, we believe in transparent, straightforward pricing. You only pay for successful transactions, with no setup fees, monthly charges, or hidden costs. Our pricing scales with your business growth.

Whether you're processing ₦10,000 or ₦10,000,000 per month, you get the same reliable service and competitive rates. Volume discounts automatically apply as your transaction volume increases.

  • Pay-as-you-go: No monthly commitments or setup fees
  • Volume discounts: Lower rates as you grow
  • Transparent fees: See exactly what you'll pay before processing
  • No hidden charges: What you see is what you pay
  • 24/7 support included: Free technical and customer support

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about PyPay pricing
How are fees calculated?
Fees are calculated as a percentage of the transaction amount plus a fixed fee. For example, a ₦10,000 transaction on the Starter plan would cost ₦200 (1.5% of ₦10,000 = ₦150 + ₦50 fixed fee).
When do I get paid?
Settlement times vary by plan: Starter (24-48 hours), Business (12-24 hours), Enterprise (Instant). Funds are transferred directly to your registered bank account automatically.
Are there any setup fees?
No, there are no setup fees for any plan. The Starter plan is completely free to get started. Business and Enterprise plans have monthly fees but no additional setup costs.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and you'll only pay for the time spent on each plan (prorated if changing mid-month).
What payment methods do you support?
We support cards (local and international), bank transfers, USSD, mobile money, and QR codes. Different payment methods have different fee structures as shown in the fee breakdown table.
Do you offer custom pricing for NGOs/Non-profits?
Yes, we offer special rates for registered non-profit organizations, educational institutions, and government agencies. Contact our sales team with your registration details to learn more.
